# Welcome to Tuxborn!

Welcome to the Tuxborn wiki! This collection of pages includes important information about the Tuxborn modpack for Skyrim: how to install and play the modpack, the Tuxborn team, frequently asked questions, playthrough content recommendations, and other links you can consult for more info.

## The Tuxborn team

* [Ouroboros](https://github.com/Ouruboros) - List developer
* [Omni](https://github.com/Omni-guides) - Linux/Steam Deck compatibility and Guide creator
* [Annathepiper](https://github.com/annathepiper) - Community helper for Steam Deck and wiki writer
* TSP - Community helper for PC

## Main topics covered on this wiki

* [Additional player resources](Additional-player-resources/): Miscellaneous additional resources for players.
* [Base game content in Tuxborn](Base-game-content-in-Tuxborn/): Overview of how base game content is changed in Tuxborn. Includes main quest, College of Winterhold, Thieves Guild, Dawnguard, Dragonborn, etc.
* [Content and quests](Content-and-quests/): General overview of content and quests information. Includes armor and weapons, spells, means of teleportation, player homes added by mods, recommendations on starting levels for quest content, etc.
* [Followers](Followers/): Information on followers available in Tuxborn, from high-level info to in-depth looks.
* [Frequently Asked Questions](Frequently-Asked-Questions/): FAQs on a variety of topics, look here if none of the other links covers what you're looking for.
* [Help with modding Tuxborn](Help-with-modding-Tuxborn/): Information you should know if you want to make any Rule 11 changes to Tuxborn, including NPC merges, replacing Community Shaders with ENB, setting up to play in ultra-wide, etc.

* [Known quest‐blocking bugs in Tuxborn](Known-quest‐blocking-bugs-in-Tuxborn/): Running list of known issues in Tuxborn content that could possibly break a quest in progress. Includes known workarounds where applicable.
